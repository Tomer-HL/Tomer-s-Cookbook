Turkish Kabab

Ingredients
- 1.3 lbs / 600g fresh ground beef (or a mix of beef and lamb)
- 1 large onion, peeled and finely grated or chopped
- A bundle of parsley, finely chopped
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp baharat spice
- Optional: a handful of finely chopped pine nuts or pistachios

Instructions
Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix well until fully combined and uniform. Refrigerate the mixture for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.

Wet your hands with water or oil. Take a portion of the meat mixture (about the size of a golf ball) and shape it onto a flat skewer or form into an oval patty by hand.

Grill on a hot grill or pan over medium-high heat for about 4-5 minutes per side, until nicely browned and cooked through.

Serve with pita, tahini, Israeli salad, and pickles. You can also serve over a bed of rice.

Description
Turkish kabab is a beloved street food across the Middle East and Israel. The key to a great kabab is using quality fresh ground meat and not over-mixing. Adding a portion of ground lamb gives a richer, more traditional flavor. Best served fresh off the grill.
