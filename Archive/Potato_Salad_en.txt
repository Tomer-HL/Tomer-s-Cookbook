Potato Salad with Mayonnaise

Ingredients
- 6-7 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 3/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tbsp white wine vinegar or lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 3 stalks celery, finely chopped
- 1 small red onion, finely diced
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley or dill, chopped
- Optional: 2 hard boiled eggs, chopped
- Optional: 3-4 pickles, diced

Instructions
Place the potato cubes in a pot of cold salted water. Bring to a boil and cook for 12-15 minutes until the potatoes are tender but not falling apart. Drain and let cool for 15 minutes.

In a large bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, salt, pepper and paprika.

Add the cooled potatoes to the bowl along with the celery, red onion, parsley and any optional ingredients. Gently fold everything together until the potatoes are well coated.

Taste and adjust seasoning. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to develop.

Serve cold or at room temperature.

Description
A classic creamy potato salad that works as a perfect side dish for grilled meats, schnitzel or any festive spread. It gets better as it sits in the fridge, so it is ideal to prepare a day ahead. The combination of mayonnaise, mustard and a touch of vinegar gives it a perfectly balanced tangy flavor.
