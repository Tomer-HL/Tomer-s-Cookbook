Arayes - Lebanese/Syrian Stuffed Pita

Ingredients
- 6 pitas, sliced in half
- Olive oil for brushing
- Optional: fresh rosemary or thyme

For the meat mixture:
- 600g / 1.3 lbs fresh ground beef (or 450g beef + 225g ground lamb)
- A bundle of parsley, finely chopped
- 1 large onion, peeled and finely chopped
- 1.5 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
- Optional: a handful of finely chopped almonds, pistachios, or pine nuts
- Optional: 1/2 tsp baharat spice

Instructions
Prepare the meat mixture: place all meat ingredients in a bowl and mix until uniform. Divide the mixture into 12 equal parts.

Fill the pita halves with the meat mixture, pressing well to keep the edges void of filling. You can cut into quarters if you wish.

Brush with olive oil on all sides of the pita (optionally sauté the oil with rosemary and thyme first). Roast on a grill or pan: place the pita halves with the opening facing down and roast until the meat is browned, then turn to the other side until also golden.

Transfer to a preheated oven at 350°F / 180°C for 5 minutes, until the meat is fully cooked and the pita is crispy. If cooking on a grill, lower the heat so it browns without burning.

Description
Arayes is one of the most famous street foods in Israel, made from pita stuffed with spiced ground meat and grilled until crispy. It is recommended to serve with tahini. Adding a mix of ground lamb to the beef gives a richer, more authentic flavor.
