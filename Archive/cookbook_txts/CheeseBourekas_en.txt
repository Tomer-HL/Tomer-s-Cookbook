Cheese Bourekas

Ingredients
- 1 pack of puff pastry
- 1 egg, beaten (for brushing)
- Sesame seeds for topping

For the cheese filling:
- 250g / 9oz Bulgarian white cheese or feta, crumbled
- 250g / 9oz ricotta or cottage cheese
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Optional: 2 tbsp fresh dill or parsley, chopped

Instructions
Preheat the oven to 350°F / 180°C. Cover a baking pan with parchment paper.

Prepare the filling: mix together the crumbled white cheese, ricotta, eggs, pepper and herbs (if using) until well combined.

With a knife or pizza cutter, cut the pastry into 10x10cm / 4"x4" squares.

Place a teaspoon or two of the cheese filling in the center of each pastry square and fold into a triangle shape. Seal the edges firmly with a fork.

Place the bourekas on the parchment paper, brush generously with beaten egg and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Description
Cheese bourekas are the most beloved version of this iconic Israeli pastry. Crispy on the outside, soft and cheesy on the inside — they are perfect for breakfast, brunch, or as a snack any time of day. Traditionally served with a hard boiled egg and Israeli salad on the side.
