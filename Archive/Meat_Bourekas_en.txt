Meat Bourekas

Ingredients
- 1 pack of puff pastry
- 1 egg, beaten (for brushing)
- Sesame seeds for topping

For the meat filling:
- 1 lb / 450g ground beef
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- Optional: 1 hard boiled egg, chopped

Instructions
Prepare the filling: heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté the onion until golden. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it apart, until fully browned. Season with salt, pepper, cumin and paprika. Remove from heat and let cool completely before filling the pastry.

Preheat the oven to 350°F / 180°C. Cover a baking pan with parchment paper.

With a knife or pizza cutter, cut the pastry into 10x10cm / 4"x4" squares.

Place a teaspoon or two of the meat filling in the center of each pastry square and close into a triangle shape. Seal the edges firmly with a fork.

Place the bourekas on the parchment paper, brush with beaten egg and sprinkle generously with sesame seeds.

Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown. Wait about 10 minutes to cool before eating.

Description
Bourekas are one of the most iconic Israeli pastries, with roots in the Sephardic Jewish communities of Turkey and the Balkans. They come in many fillings — cheese, potato, mushroom, spinach, eggplant and meat. Meat bourekas are a hearty and satisfying version, perfect for Shabbat or any festive occasion.
