Roasted Eggplant Salad - Baba Ganoush

Ingredients
- 2 medium eggplants
- 1-2 crushed garlic cloves (or 1/2 tsp garlic powder)
- 2 tbsp fresh squeezed lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
- A pinch of ground black pepper
- A dash (1/8 tsp) of cumin
- 1-2 tbsp raw tahini
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Fresh cilantro or parsley to garnish

Instructions
Roast the eggplants on a grill on all sides until the peel turns completely black, about 15-25 minutes for a medium eggplant. You can wrap the eggplant in foil. Make sure there is a small opening cut at the bottom so it does not explode from the heat.

Take them off the grill when they are very soft and have lost about half their volume. Stab the eggplant with a knife at the bottom and sides and place them in a colander with a plate underneath to drain for about 30 minutes to 1 hour.

Peel the eggplant skin, remove the stem, and place the flesh in a bowl. Add the squeezed lemon juice immediately so the eggplant does not turn black.

Crush the eggplant with a fork or chop thinly with a knife. Add the crushed garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, cumin and raw tahini. Mix well until uniform.

Add parsley or cilantro to garnish. Serve at room temperature.

Description
A simple and easy recipe for roasted eggplant salad with tahini (Baba Ganoush), which makes a great dip for any BBQ, Shabbat, holiday or festive meal. Roasting the eggplant on the grill gives it the most delicious smoky flavor. You can also roast the eggplant in the oven as an alternative.
