Juicy Kebab

Ingredients
 1.3 lb (600 g) fresh ground beef (or a beef & lamb mix) – preferably from short ribs (asado cut)
 1 large onion, peeled and grated or finely chopped
 1 bunch parsley or cilantro, finely chopped
 1 ½ tsp salt
 ½ tsp baking soda
 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
 ½ tsp ground cumin
 ½ tsp ground cinnamon
 Optional: ¼ tsp baharat spice blend
 Optional: a handful of finely chopped pine nuts or pistachios

Instructions
1. Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix היטב just until combined (avoid overmixing).
2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to develop.
3. Wet your hands with water or oil. Take a portion of the meat mixture (about the size of a golf ball) and shape it onto a flat skewer, or form into an oval shape by hand.
4. Grill over medium-high heat or cook in a skillet for about 4–5 minutes per side, until nicely browned and cooked through.
5. Serve with pita, tahini, Israeli salad, and pickles. Can also be served over rice.

Description
Turkish-style kebab is a beloved street food across the Middle East and Israel. The key to a great kebab is using fresh, high-quality meat and not overworking the mixture. Adding ground lamb gives a richer, more traditional flavor. Best served hot straight off the grill.
