Moroccan_Matbucha

A classic dish you can easily make at home.
Matbucha is a classic Moroccan tomato and pepper spread that is slow-cooked to develop a deep, rich, and concentrated flavor. The long cooking time and patience are the key to achieving its perfect texture and taste. It’s excellent as a dip with fresh bread, served alongside hummus and salads, or as a flavorful addition to any meal.
🕒 Time: 1 1/2 hours
🍽️ Servings: 6
🧑‍🍳 Level: Medium

Ingredients

  • 6 medium-large ripe tomatoes or 800 g high-quality crushed tomato (recommended: MUTTI)
  • 2 red peppers, roasted, peeled and chopped
  • 6–8 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 5–6 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp hot paprika
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2–1 tsp chili flakes or 1 fresh chili, finely chopped (to taste)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste at the end)

Instructions

  1. Roasting the peppers
    Place the peppers directly over a gas flame or under the oven broiler. Turn occasionally until the skin is fully charred on all sides. Transfer to a sealed bag for 10 minutes,then peel, remove seeds, and chop.
  2. Start cooking
    Heat olive oil in a wide, heavy pan over medium-low heat. Add the garlic and sauté gently for 30–60 seconds, until fragrant (do not burn).
  3. Add ingredients
    Add the chopped tomatoes or tomato paste, along with the peppers, hot paprika, sweet paprika, cumin, chili, salt, and sugar. Mix well.
  4. Slow cooking
    Cook uncovered over medium-low heat for 45–60 minutes. Stir every 5–10 minutes to prevent sticking.
  5. The matbucha is ready when
    - Most of the liquid has evaporated.
    - The texture is thick and jam-like.
    - You begin to see slight oil separation.
  6. Taste and finish
    Taste and adjust seasoning (you can add more salt / chili / a bit of sugar as needed).
    Optional: Add one fresh crushed garlic clove at the end of cooking and mix.
  7. Cooling and serving
    Let the matbucha cool and serve at room temperature or cold.
    Storage: Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
